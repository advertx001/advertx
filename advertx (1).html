About Our Services

What's the minimum engagement period?
+
We recommend a minimum 3-month engagement for ad campaigns to gather sufficient data and optimise effectively. Website and branding projects are typically 4–8 week one-time projects. Most clients stay long-term — 98% renew — because the results keep improving.
Do I keep ownership of everything you build?
+
Absolutely. You own 100% of everything — your Google Ads account, Meta Business Manager, website files, brand assets, and all creative. We never lock you into proprietary systems. Everything we create stays with you forever.
How quickly can ads go live?
+
For new clients, we typically launch within 7–14 days — including strategy, creative production, account setup, and compliance review. For urgent launches, we can compress to 72 hours for basic campaigns. Quality always comes first.
Can you work with our existing agency or in-house team?
+
Yes. We frequently work alongside in-house teams or other specialists. We can own specific channels or serve as a full-service extension of your team. We're flexible and collaborative.
What reporting do we receive?
+
Every client gets a live Looker Studio dashboard updated daily, weekly email performance summaries, and a monthly strategy review call. Full transparency, always.

FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

Are there any setup fees?
+
No hidden setup fees. The monthly price is all-inclusive. For custom enterprise projects, we'll provide a clear itemised proposal before any work begins.
Can I upgrade or downgrade plans?
+
Absolutely. You can upgrade anytime — we'll prorate the difference. Downgrades take effect from the next billing cycle. We're flexible and understand business needs change.
What's included in the ad spend cap?
+
The ad spend cap refers to the budget we manage on your behalf in the ad platform. Our management fee is included in the monthly plan price. If you need a higher cap, we offer add-ons.
Is there a contract lock-in?
+
We recommend 3-month minimum for performance campaigns to show real results. After that, you're on a rolling monthly basis. No long-term lock-ins, no exit penalties.
